About Us

We’re on a mission to make you snack healthy so you feel good throughout the day. We are parents, daughters, sons, spouses, gym-goers, pet and food lovers. We discovered the elegant nutrition of foxnuts by accident - when finding healthy snacks for our kids, giving soft food to grandparents and looking for a better munchy alternative for movie nights. We would love for you to switch to foxnuts - good for you, good for your family.

Our Product

Sustainability

We source our foxnuts from small farmers in the historically and culturally rich state of Bihar. They are grown keeping in mind the best sustainable practices. Our farmers have access to all resources to live a life of dignity. A portion of our profits is given back to this community to pay it forward.
